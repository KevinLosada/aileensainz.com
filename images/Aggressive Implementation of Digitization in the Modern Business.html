Title:
Aggressive Implementation of Digitization in the Modern Business
Author:
Gijic, Nebojsa; Dimitrijevic, Oliver; Jovic-Bogdanovic, Adriana
Author Affiliation:
U Union Nikola Tesla; U Union Nikola Tesla; U Union Nikola Tesla
Source:
Ekonomika, 2014, v. 60, iss. 4, pp. 227-39
Publication Date:
2014
Abstract:
The global economic crisis business environment pressures companies to streamline their operations, thus forcing them to significantly speed up the process of digitizing, creating a kind of digital markets. It becomes clear that those who recognize the moment and who are willing to think in a modern and unconventional way in terms of innovation, customer and business partners engagement, corporative organization strategies, business models and the role of technology in their business enterprise will be those who will be well-positioned in this new market, which will enable them to have significant revenue growth. On the other hand, there is a significant business risk of fatal business loss in case of inaction, slowness, neglect and lack of understanding of this very important process in the business.
